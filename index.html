Best Things to Do in Belgrade

Knez Mihailova Street

Main pedestrian zone with cafés, shops, and historic buildings.

Kalemegdan Fortress

Best sunset spot in the city with river views and parks.

Skadarlija

Bohemian street with restaurants and nightlife.

Budget Tips

Use Public Transport

Cheap buses and trams across the city.

Stay Local

Dorćol, Vračar, Zemun = better value.

Walk More

Most attractions are close together.
