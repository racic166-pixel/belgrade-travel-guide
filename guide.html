3 Days in Belgrade 🇷🇸

This is a simple, practical itinerary for visiting Belgrade. It focuses on realistic routes, local areas, cafés, and relaxed pacing — not rushed tourist checklists.

Day 1 — Central Belgrade

Old Town & Main Sights

Start your day in the city center around Knez Mihailova, then walk toward Kalemegdan Fortress for views over the rivers.

In the evening, Skadarlija is a good area for traditional restaurants and a slower atmosphere.

Day 2 — Riverside & Local Areas

Zemun & Danube Side

Zemun has a different feel compared to the center — quieter streets, riverside cafés, and more local life.

  • Walk along the Danube promenade
  • Stop at small cafés near the river
  • Explore older residential streets

Day 3 — Relaxed Day

Ada Ciganlija & Dorćol

This day is for slower exploring. Ada Ciganlija is a green area where locals go to relax, walk, or sit by the water.

Later, Dorćol is a good area for cafés and quiet streets.

Practical Notes

Belgrade is easier to explore when you don’t rush. Walking is often the best way to experience the city center, and bakeries are a simple option for affordable meals.
