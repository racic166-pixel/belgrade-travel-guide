Is Belgrade Expensive? 🇷🇸 (2026 Travel Cost Guide)

No — Belgrade is one of the most affordable capital cities in Europe. You can travel comfortably on a budget while still enjoying food, nightlife, and attractions.

🍽️ Food Prices in Belgrade

Eating out is cheap compared to Western Europe. Local bakeries and restaurants offer very affordable meals.

Average cost: €3–€10 per meal depending on place.

🏨 Accommodation Prices

Hotels and apartments are generally affordable, especially outside peak season.

Check Hotel Prices

🚍 Transport Costs

Public transport is very cheap and covers most of the city.

💸 Daily Budget Example

  • Budget traveler: €30–€50/day
  • Comfort traveler: €60–€100/day
  • Luxury: €120+/day

📍 Final Answer

Belgrade is NOT expensive compared to most European capitals. It’s a strong destination for budget travelers.
