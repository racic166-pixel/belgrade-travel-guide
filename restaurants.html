Best Restaurants in Belgrade 🇷🇸 (2026 Food Guide)

Belgrade has a strong food culture with affordable prices, traditional Serbian dishes, bakeries, and modern cafés. You can eat well without spending much.

🍽️ Traditional Serbian Food

Local restaurants serve grilled meat, stews, and traditional Balkan dishes at very affordable prices.

🥐 Bakeries (Fast & Cheap Food)

Bakeries are everywhere in Belgrade and are the cheapest way to eat. Popular items include burek, pastries, and sandwiches.

☕ Best Cafés in Belgrade

Dorćol and Vračar have many modern cafés with coffee culture similar to Western Europe but cheaper.

🌊 Riverside Restaurants (Zemun Area)

Zemun offers restaurants along the river with great views and relaxed atmosphere.

💡 Budget Food Tips

  • Eat at bakeries for cheap meals
  • Avoid tourist-only restaurants in center
  • Try local grill spots for best value

📍 Plan Your Trip

Combine food exploration with sightseeing for the best experience in Belgrade.
