Where to Stay in Belgrade 🇷🇸

Choosing the right area in Belgrade makes a huge difference in your trip. This guide explains the best neighborhoods, hotel options, and apartment areas so you can stay in the right place based on your travel style and budget.

🏙️ Best Areas to Stay in Belgrade

  • Stari Grad — best for first-time visitors, close to all main attractions
  • Dorćol — cafés, local vibe, modern and relaxed
  • Vračar — residential, quiet, good restaurants
  • Zemun — riverside area, peaceful and scenic

🧭 How to Choose the Right Area

  • If it’s your first visit → stay in Stari Grad
  • If you want cafés & local life → Dorćol
  • If you want peace & value → Vračar
  • If you want scenery & calm → Zemun
